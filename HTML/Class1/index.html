How to Become a Frontend Developer

Who is a Frontend Developer?

A Frontend Developer is a professional who specializes in creating the user-facing part of a website - everything a person sees and interacts within their browser. They are the architects of the digital experience, responsible for turning a static design into a living, breathing, and interactive webpage.
